About our company

Komcluez Marketing
and Consulting Pvt. Ltd.

Komcluez is a consulting business startup by PRAYAS started in 2020. The basic motive of komcluez is to encourage youth and budding entrepreneurs to start their own businesses and to bring them under one roof .Komcluez provides these new businesses with suitable market strategies and time to time analysis to boost their profits ,in return the entrepreneur or vendor or businessman has to take prayas membership which authenticates the person as a member of a reputed organization and makes him/her eligible to do business without much third party disturbances as that of contracting where most of the profit isn't shared directly with the vendor /businessman / entrepreneur.

Business strategy growth

We aim to help businesses and budding companies by providing them with suitable business strategies and real time analysis while also generating employment for people in various sectors with a possible target of 2000 employees and 50 companies by next year!

Visionary idea

We believe that socio-economic development has great potential to impact economic growth and hence strive to achieve it.
